Day Care, Kindergarten, Nursery & Preschool Programs

Age-appropriate learning paths for early confidence, care, creativity and classroom readiness in Rewa.

Day Care

Safe daily day care and daycare in Rewa with supervised routines, rest, play and parent communication.

Pre Play

Gentle first-school exposure through movement, stories, music and guided interaction.

Play Group

Foundational social, language and motor skills through joyful activity-based learning.

Nursery

Early literacy, numbers, expression, habits and classroom confidence for young learners.

Junior KG

Kindergarten readiness for reading, writing, speaking, counting and independent routines.

Senior KG

Confident kindergarten preparation for primary school with stronger concepts and communication.

Primary Classes

CBSE-aligned academic growth with activity, discipline, values and practical exposure.

After-School Support

Creative activities, homework support and care routines for parent-friendly scheduling.

Age-Appropriate Learning Path

  • Early years: children learn through play, rhythm, stories, sensory activities and safe social interaction in a preschool environment.
  • Kindergarten: children build reading readiness, number sense, writing habits, confidence and classroom independence.
  • Primary classes: children move into structured subjects with practical learning, activities, values and communication skills.
  • Care support: day care, daycare and after-school routines help Rewa parents with safe supervision beyond regular class hours.

Why Parents Choose Our Programs

  • Activity-based teaching for better understanding and happier participation.
  • Child-friendly classrooms, play areas, smart learning facilities and supervised campus routines.
  • Regular admission support for class selection, fee guidance, documents and transport confirmation.
  • Balanced focus on academics, values, confidence, creativity, hygiene and physical development.
