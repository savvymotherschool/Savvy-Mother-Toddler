CBSE Curriculum

A balanced learning plan that supports academics, values, creativity, health and confident communication.

Foundational Learning

Concepts are introduced through stories, activities, observation, conversation and hands-on classroom experiences.

Activity Based

Play, art, music, movement, games and practical work help children understand ideas naturally.

Values & Life Skills

Daily routines build discipline, kindness, independence, confidence, hygiene and respect for others.

CBSE Curriculum Focus

  • Language & Communication: Listening, speaking, vocabulary, phonics, reading readiness, writing readiness and confident expression.
  • Numeracy & Thinking: Number sense, shapes, patterns, sorting, comparison, sequencing and early problem-solving.
  • Environmental Awareness: Family, community helpers, nature, seasons, plants, animals, safety and healthy habits.
  • Creative Development: Drawing, colouring, craft, music, dance, role play and storytelling.
  • Physical Development: Indoor play, outdoor play, yoga, games, balancing, coordination and fine motor activities.
  • Digital & Smart Learning: Age-appropriate digital content, visual learning and guided classroom technology.

Class-Wise Approach

Level Learning Approach Key Outcomes
Day Care / Pre Play Care routines, sensory play, rhymes, stories and social comfort. Settling, confidence, sharing, listening and basic self-help habits.
Play / Nursery Theme-based activities, pre-writing, pre-number work and creative play. Language readiness, motor control, curiosity and classroom participation.
Junior KG / Senior KG Phonics, early reading, writing practice, number concepts and project work. School readiness, communication, counting, writing confidence and independence.
Class 1 to Class 7 Structured academics supported by activities, digital tools and regular practice. Concept clarity, discipline, creativity, responsibility and all-round growth.

Parent Note

Detailed books, notebooks, activities and assessment routines are shared by the school office as per the current academic session.
