Refund Policy

Clear information about form fees, payments and refund-related requests.

Form Fee

Admission form fees are non-refundable once paid.

Receipt Record

Keep receipts and confirm payment details with the office.

Office Decision

Adjustments or exceptions require school management approval.

Non-Refundable Payments

  • Admission form fees and form-processing charges are not refundable after payment.
  • Fees paid for services already used, processed or reserved may not be refundable.
  • Parents are requested to verify child details, class, contact information and required documents before form submission.

Refund or Adjustment Requests

  • Any request must be submitted to the school office with receipt details and the reason for the request.
  • Approved refunds or adjustments, if any, will be processed according to school management rules.
  • Bank or transaction charges, if applicable, may not be refundable.

Professional Note

Please read all admission and fee information carefully before making payment. For clarification, parents should contact the school office before submitting the form or paying any fee.
