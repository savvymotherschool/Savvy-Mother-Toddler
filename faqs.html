FAQs

Answers to common parent questions about admissions, timings, care, transport and communication.

Admissions

For admission availability, age criteria and documents, parents should contact the school office or visit during working hours.

School Timings

School and day care timings can vary by class and service. The office shares final timings during admission confirmation.

Safety

The campus follows supervised entry, CCTV monitoring, hygiene checks and authorised guardian handover routines.

Common Questions

  • Is day care available?
    Yes, day care support is available with safe routines and attentive supervision.
  • Is transport available?
    Transport depends on route availability. Please confirm your location with the office.
  • Are meals provided?
    The school provides fresh and hot lunch support as per the current facility plan.
  • How are parents updated?
    Parents are informed through school communication, phone calls and parent-teacher interactions.
  • Can we visit the campus?
    Yes, school visits can be planned during working hours after contacting the office.
