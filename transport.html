Transport

Safe, supervised and coordinated transport support for children on approved school routes.

Route Support

Availability is confirmed by location and approved school routes.

Supervision

Pick-up and drop are coordinated by trained staff and attendants.

Communication

For transport support, call +91 9425621360.

Transport Guidelines

  • Children are handed over only to authorised guardians.
  • Parents should be present at the stop before the scheduled time.
  • Any route or guardian change must be shared with the school office in advance.
  • Transport rules are explained during admission or route confirmation.
