About School

Learning rooted in values, creativity, and future skills.

Savvy Mother Toddler School Rewa creates a caring environment where children grow through academics, discipline, culture, technology, yoga, and practical life learning.

LEARN MORE ABOUT OUR WORK

Your dreams are tied with a key, Our School unlocks it.

The aim of Savvy Mother Toddler School Rewa is to provide quality education through the mental, physical, spiritual, vital, and psychological aspects of human life, helping children become enlightened and confident learners from preschool to primary classes.

The school deals with data science, coding, computing, A.I., and machine learning. For physical activities, yoga including asanas and pranayam, P.T., and indoor and outdoor games are included. For vital and spiritual enhancement, Sanskrit mantras, Geeta chanting, Shree Ramcharit Manas, and meditation are core activities of the school.

We shape the new generation with modern technology, orient them toward upcoming skills, and bring out creative talent through multiple modes of education. Our approach provides a culmination of modern learning and Vedic culture, where mental, physical, vital, psychological, and spiritual growth remain the fundamental parts of school life.

Teaching in the school is not only based on bookish knowledge. It is based on practical knowledge of every subject. Students are helped to brighten their inner immense power and knowledge through yogic activity so that they can face any challenge of life and society.

Future Skills

Coding, computing, data science, A.I., and machine learning introduce children to technology-oriented learning.

Physical Growth

Yoga, pranayam, P.T., and indoor and outdoor games support strength, discipline, health, and confidence.

Values & Culture
