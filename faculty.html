Leadership Team

Meet the core team guiding the vision and day-to-day learning at our campus.

Dr. Sujay Singh

Director
Ph.D. in Education, 23+ years of experience in school leadership.

Dr. Singh leads Savvy Mother Toddler School with a strong focus on holistic development, academic excellence and values-based education. His vision is to create a joyful, safe and future-ready learning environment for every child.

Mrs. Nirmala Singh

Assistant Managing Director
18+ years in academic administration and parent engagement.

Mrs. Singh closely works with teachers, staff and parents to ensure smooth day-to-day functioning of the school. Her warm and approachable style helps families feel supported and connected to the school community.

Ajeet Singh

Principal
M.Ed., 15+ years in school management and teaching.

As Principal, Mr. Singh leads the academic team, designs child-centered teaching plans and ensures every classroom follows age-appropriate, joyful and effective learning methods.

Mrs. Rashmi Singh

Academic Head
12+ years of experience in early childhood curriculum design.

Mrs. Rashmi curates engaging lesson plans, activity-based modules and teacher training sessions to keep learning fun, structured and aligned with early childhood best practices.
