Director's Message

Guiding Young Minds With Purpose

“The first principle of true teaching is that nothing can be taught.” – Sri Aurobindo

Dear Parents, Students, and Well-Wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Savvy Mother School, Rewa – a place where young minds are nurtured, values are instilled, and dreams are given wings.

At Savvy Mother School, we believe that education is a lifelong journey. Our mission is not just academic excellence, but also the holistic development of each student — intellectually, emotionally, socially, and morally. We foster a learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.

We take pride in our dedicated faculty, innovative teaching methods, and a curriculum that blends traditional values with modern education. Our school is a community where every child is known, valued, and supported.

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains the same: To provide a safe, inclusive, and inspiring space for every learner. Together, with the support of parents and guardians, we aim to build a brighter future for our children and the society at large.

Thank you for placing your trust in Savvy Mother School.

Warm regards,

Dr. Sujay Singh Director
