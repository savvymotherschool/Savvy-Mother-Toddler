Parent Quick Answers

  • Which documents are required for admission?
    Parents should keep child ID, family ID, Aadhaar copies, birth certificate, Samagra ID and other listed records ready for office verification.
  • Is admission open for preschool, daycare and kindergarten in Rewa?
    Parents can submit the admission enquiry or contact the school office for current seat availability in daycare, preschool, nursery, Junior KG, Senior KG and primary classes.
  • Are photographs required?
    Yes, recent coloured photographs of the child, father and mother are required during admission verification.
  • Can parents visit the campus before admission?
    Yes, parents can contact the school office to plan a campus visit, discuss class fit, fees, daycare support and transport guidance.
  • Is the admission form fee refundable?
    No, the admission form fee is non-refundable. Please review all details before submitting the form.
