Campus Facilities

Child-friendly classrooms, safe play zones and thoughtful services that make every day comfortable, secure and joyful for our little learners.

Our Distinctive Features

A quick look at the key features that define our vibrant learning environment

BRAIN SCIENCE LAB (BRAINYWOOD)

After Pune Army School and Dehradun Army School, first time in Rewa, Savvy Mother International School activates brain and memory, where students can unlock their true potential.

DATA SCIENCE & AI CLASSES

First in Rewa to offer Data Science & AI classes - preparing students for careers of the future through practical, project-based learning.

ABACUS CLASSES

Abacus classes help children build concentration, number sense, memory and confidence through engaging practice designed for young learners.

DAILY YOGA & MINDFUL MOVEMENT

Daily yoga sessions help children build flexibility, balance, calm breathing, focus and healthy habits through gentle age-appropriate movement.
