Inside Our Campus

Every corner of Savvy Mother Toddler School is designed around safety, comfort and learning.

Classrooms

Airy, Child-Friendly Rooms

Bright, air‑conditioned classrooms with colourful learning corners, soft flooring and child-sized furniture to keep children comfortable and focused.

Each classroom is equipped with CCTV monitoring, AC and emergency exits.

Transport

Safe School Transport

GPS-enabled vehicles with trained drivers and female attendants to ensure safe pick-up and drop for every child on designated routes.

Daily sanitisation and thorough attendance checks are maintained.

Meals

Healthy Snack Corner

Hygienic serving area where children enjoy nutritious, child-friendly snacks and learn good eating habits and table manners together.

Weekly menu plans are shared with parents in advance.

Hygiene

Child-Safe Washrooms

Separate, low-height washrooms for children with non-slippery flooring, sensor taps and regular cleaning schedules throughout the day.

Hygiene is checked by staff multiple times daily.

Medical

First-Aid & Basic Care

On-campus first-aid support, basic health checks and tie-ups with nearby doctors to handle minor concerns quickly and responsibly.

Parents are informed immediately in case of any health issue.

Security

24x7 CCTV Monitoring

All key areas including entrances, corridors, classrooms and play zones are monitored through CCTV to ensure complete safety.

Entry and exit are strictly monitored by security staff.

Note: Entry of outsiders is strictly restricted. Children are handed over only to authorised guardians as per school records.
