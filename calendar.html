Academic Calendar

Important school routines, celebrations and parent interaction windows for a smooth academic year.

School Session

The academic session is planned with age-appropriate classroom learning, activities, celebrations and regular parent updates.

Monthly Activities

Theme days, festivals, health awareness activities, storytelling, art and play-based learning events are scheduled throughout the year.

Parent Meetings

Parent-teacher interactions are held periodically to discuss progress, habits, confidence and classroom participation.

Calendar Highlights

  • April - June: New session, settling routines, summer activities and early assessments.
  • July - September: Monsoon themes, Independence Day, creative activities and parent interaction.
  • October - December: Festival celebrations, winter activities, revision and performance practice.
  • January - March: Annual events, progress review, readiness activities and session completion.

Parent Note

Final dates for holidays, celebrations and meetings are shared by the school office through official communication.
